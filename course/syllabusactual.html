    Section Course Information

    Subsection Course Description

    A thorough introduction to the application of statistics to the sciences. Topics include descriptive statistics, probability distributions, and a strong emphasis on regression and hypothesis testing, including t-tests, chi-square tests, and analysis of variance. Emphasis is placed on analyzing data to select appropriate inferential methods. Statistical software is used throughout.
    🔗
    🔗

    Subsection Prerequisites

    A grade of C (2.00) or higher in MATH 123 Introduction to Functions or eligibility to enroll in MATH 180 Precalculus.
    🔗
    🔗
    🔗

    Section Course Learning Outcomes

    By the end of the course, you will be able to:
    🔗
    1. Turn research problems into statistical questions.
      🔗
      🔗
    2. Identify study structure and its impact on conclusions.
      🔗
      🔗
    3. Construct and interpret graphical representations of data.
      🔗
      🔗
    4. Compute and interpret summary statistics in plain language.
      🔗
      🔗
    5. Select and apply appropriate statistical methods.
      🔗
      🔗
    6. Communicate results of statistical analyses effectively.
      🔗
      🔗
    7. Use statistical software for analysis and visualization.
      🔗
      🔗
